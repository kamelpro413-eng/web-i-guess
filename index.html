Getting Started

Up and running
in 3 steps

Configure Ticket Logger in under two minutes — no experience needed.

01

Set your logging channel

Tell the bot where to send ticket logs. Replace IDCHANNEL with your channel's ID.

!loggerchannel IDCHANNEL
02

Assign your staff roles

Pick which roles can trigger ticket logging. Add as many as you need — there is no limit.

!loggerrole @Role1 @Role2 ...
03

You're live 🎉

The bot automatically logs tickets and tracks staff activity from here on out. Zero maintenance needed.
