01 — What We Collect

Ticket Logger collects only the minimum data required to function. Here's exactly what that is:

DataDescriptionRequired?
Server IDThe unique identifier of your Discord server.Yes
Channel IDThe channel you configure for logs.Yes
Role IDsThe roles you assign as logging triggers.Yes
User IDsIDs of staff members who interact with tickets.Yes

We do not collect names, emails, message content, or any other personal information.

02 — Why We Collect It

Each piece of data serves a specific purpose:

  • Server & Channel IDs — to know where to deliver your ticket logs.
  • Role IDs — to determine which users should trigger logging.
  • User IDs — to attribute ticket actions to the correct staff member in logs.

We collect nothing beyond what's strictly necessary to provide the service.

03 — Data Sharing

We take data sharing seriously. Here's our simple policy:

  • We do not sell your data. Ever.
  • We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not share your data with any external services unless required by law.

Your server's data stays between you and Ticket Logger.

04 — Data Retention

We store configuration data (server ID, channel ID, role IDs) for as long as the bot is in your server. When you remove the bot, we will delete your server's configuration data within 30 days.

Log entries are delivered to your Discord channel and are not stored on our servers beyond delivery.

05 — Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about your server.
  • Request deletion of your server's data at any time by contacting us.
  • Remove the bot from your server at any time, which stops all data collection immediately.

06 — Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

07 — Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, want to request data deletion, or have a concern, please reach out to us via Discord. We'll respond as soon as possible.
